Look up courses by a CRN

Don't know where to find your course CRNs? Go to the Wellesley Course Browser to find courses you are interested in!

    1. To start type in a CRN and hit 'Submit'.
    2. You can then select 'Add Data' to add the course to your calendar.
    3. To delete a course from your calendar, type in the CRN of the course, hit 'Submit' followed by 'Delete Data'.
